About Us

In Bangladesh, farmers face unfair prices due to syndicates and limited market information, while consumers pay high prices without quality assurance. Our company, Shochho krishi, enhances the supply chain by ensuring fair pricing, fresh produce, and reducing waste through improved transport, storage, and distribution.

Our Promise

Transparency

Sustainability

Supporting contract farming, sustainable practices, and better storage.

How It Works

Our system connects farmers directly with consumers using modern technologies. From crop production to storage and delivery, every step is monitored through blockchain and IoT. This ensures fair returns for farmers, fresh products for customers, and a sustainable cycle for the environment.
